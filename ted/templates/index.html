OpenTED releases updated data extracts from the European procurement database, Tenders Electronic Daily (TED). The data on this site is machine-readable CSV, ready for examination by journalists and others. Since the EU Publications Office does not make such a form of the data available, the content has been generated through an automated scraper script, which downloads and analyses the TED web site.

To find out more about this effort, visit OpenTED.org.
